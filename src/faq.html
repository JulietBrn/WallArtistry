Frequently Asked Questions

What is the delivery time for orders?

The delivery time depends on your location and the type of order. Typically, it takes 5-7 business days for standard orders and 10-15 business days for custom orders.

Do you offer international shipping?

Currently, we only ship within India. We are working on expanding our services to international locations in the near future.

Can I return or exchange an item?

Yes, we offer a 7-day return or exchange policy for standard items. Custom orders are non-refundable. Please refer to our return policy for more details.

How can I place a custom order?

You can place a custom order by filling out the order form on our website or contacting us via email at wallartistry@gmail.com with your requirements.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards, UPI payments, and net banking. Cash on delivery is also available for select locations.

Are the artworks framed?

Yes, most of our artworks come with high-quality frames. You can also choose to order unframed pieces if preferred.

Do you offer discounts for bulk orders?

Yes, we offer special discounts for bulk orders. Please contact us directly for more details and a custom quote.

How do I care for my artwork?

To maintain the quality of your artwork, avoid direct sunlight, excessive humidity, and use a soft cloth for cleaning. For framed pieces, ensure the glass is clean and intact.

Tips for Choosing Artwork for Your Home

1. Consider the Room's Style

Choose artwork that complements the overall style and color scheme of the room. For modern spaces, opt for abstract or minimalist pieces, while traditional rooms may benefit from classic or landscape art.

2. Focus on Size and Placement

Ensure the artwork is proportionate to the wall and furniture. Large pieces work well as focal points, while smaller pieces can be grouped together for a gallery effect.

3. Reflect Your Personality

Select artwork that resonates with your personal taste and interests. This will make your space feel more authentic and inviting.

4. Experiment with Colors

Use artwork to introduce pops of color or tie together existing hues in the room. Bold colors can create a statement, while neutral tones offer subtle elegance.

5. Mix and Match Styles

Don't be afraid to mix different styles and mediums, such as paintings, photographs, and prints, to create a dynamic and layered look.
